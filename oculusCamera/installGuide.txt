****
Arnold Oculus Camera deployment guide

tested with software versions:
    Maya 2015
    Arnold 1.2.0.1 (from maya help menu)
    SDK Arnold-4.2.3.1-windows

compiled 2015 by Callan Winfield
****

steps to deploy

1: copy contents of shaders to:
        C:\solidangle\mtoadeploy\2015\shaders

2: copy contents of python to:
        C:\solidangle\mtoadeploy\2015\scripts\mtoa\ui\ae

boot Maya, create a camera, look under the "Arnold" menu, select "oculusCamera" and continue on your merry rending way
